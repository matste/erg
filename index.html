About

Event Rental Group burst onto the Toronto event scene in 1989 with a mandate to supply event professionals with visionary products, exceptional service, and an impeccable attention to detail. Today, the highly skilled Exclusive team has achieved its mandate and is moving forward with a vision to continue to bring unique and innovative products to the increasingly sophisticated event scene.

Event Rental Group offers an unparalleled selection of bold china settings from classic to contemporary, exquisite seating selections, dynamic tabletop accoutrements, and dramatic presentation pieces. We strive to ensure your guests are left with a striking impression and an overwhelming satisfaction, even after the last votive candle has its final flicker.

Event Rental Group was formed in 2007, following Exclusive's acquisition of Advanced Tent Rental, Canada's premier tenting company.
